# Bodycare Yumin Project Overview

## Project Purpose
This is the official website for "手もみ整体 癒眠" (Bodycare Yumin), a Japanese massage therapy clinic. The website serves as their online presence to provide information about services, facilitate bookings, and showcase customer testimonials.

## Key Features
- Responsive design for all devices
- Service introduction and descriptions
- Access information with Google Maps integration
- Online reservation system
- Contact form functionality
- Business calendar/schedule display
- Blog system (recently added feature with admin panel)

## Target Audience
- Customers seeking massage therapy services
- People looking for relaxation and body care
- Local community members
- Potential new clients researching services

## Business Context
- Japanese massage therapy clinic
- Focus on manual therapy ("手もみ整体")
- Brand name: 癒眠 (Yumin - meaning healing sleep)
- Professional healthcare service provider
